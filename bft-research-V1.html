Analyse Reliance Q4 FY24 revenue breakdown by segment. What's driving growth?
◈

Reliance posted consolidated revenue of ₹2,32,955 crores in Q4 FY24, up 11.3% YoY. Segment breakdown:

O2C (Oil-to-Chemicals): ₹1,54,877 Cr — 66.5% of total. GRM recovered to $8.2/bbl vs $6.7 in Q4 FY23. Annual Report 2024 · pg.47

Retail (Reliance Retail): ₹68,937 Cr, up 17.9% YoY. 3,300+ new stores in FY24. JioMart GMV integration. Q4 FY24 Earnings · pg.12

Digital (Jio Platforms): ₹31,264 Cr, up 12.2% YoY. ARPU: ₹181.7. Subscribers: 489.7M. Jio Platforms Filing · pg.8

Management guides for 15–18% EBITDA growth in FY25, led by Retail expansion + Jio 5G monetisation. Mgmt Commentary · pg.3

What is the debt situation? Are they over-leveraged?
◈

Net debt stands at ₹1,19,045 crores as of March 2024. Not over-leveraged by any standard measure:

• Total Debt: ₹3,12,441 Cr | Cash: ₹1,93,396 Cr Annual Report 2024 · pg.112
• Net Debt/EBITDA: 1.1x (FY24) vs 1.8x in FY22
• Interest Coverage: 7.2x — healthy buffer Financial Filings · pg.19
• 74% of debt matures beyond 5 years — long-duration, low near-term risk
• 5G capex winding down → FCF inflection expected FY25–26
